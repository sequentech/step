Governance & Cost Control

Uncontrolled financial spend and compliance risks due to decentralized AI usage without a central strategy.

Data Breaches

Sensitive corporate data and IP leaking through uncontrolled employee interaction with public AI models.

Lack of Visibility

Inability to track, monitor, and audit all AI usage (Shadow AI) across the enterprise, creating critical blind spots.

Abusive Usage

Malicious attacks (e.g., prompt injection) targeting internal AI apps, and employees using AI for harmful purposes.
